S-au pus bazele unei industrii de utilaj petrolier – fabricând: instalații de foraj, agregate de cimentare, sape cu role, turbine de foraj, unitati de pompaj, etc.

Prin volumul productiei sale de utilaj petrolier, Romania ocupa locul al III lea in lume, iar in privinta exporturilor, locul al II lea:

Prăjini folosite la sondă, instrumente și echipamente folosite la foraj de diferite tipuri:

  • Sapa cu 3 lame

  • Sapa cu 3 role

  • Sapa cu 2 role – foraj rotativ (1940) – folosită pt. terenurile slab consolidate și aducerea la suprafață a fragmentelor de rocă dislocate. Fabricate la Intreprinderea 1 Mai din Ploiești.

The foundations of an oil equipment industry were laid - manufacturing: drilling rigs, cementing aggregates, roller hoes, drilling turbines, pumping units, etc.

By the volume of its oil equipment production, Romania occupied the 3rd place in the world, and in terms of exports, the 2nd place:

Drilling rods, drilling tools and equipment of various types:

  • Hoe with 3 blades

  • Hoe with 3 rollers

  • Hoe with 2 rollers - rotary drilling (1940) - used for poorly consolidated soils and bringing to the surface dislocated rock fragments. Manufactured at 1 Mai Enterprise in Ploiesti.
